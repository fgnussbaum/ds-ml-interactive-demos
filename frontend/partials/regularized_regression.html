Regularization: Ridge & Lasso

Regularization penalizes large coefficients to prevent overfitting — but too much shrinks the model into uselessness. Ridge (L2) and Lasso (L1) shrink differently: Ridge nudges all coefficients toward zero; Lasso drives some to exactly zero, selecting features.
